Roadmap: All tokens will be distributed for free. Our philosophy is that the market will decide our value

1. Token Airdrop to Motoko Ghost Holders

Initiating our roadmap, we will commence with an exclusive airdrop of tokens, dedicating 30% of the total allocation to Motoko Ghost Holders. This strategic move is designed to reward our early supporters and stakeholders, recognizing their pivotal role in our journey.

2. Public Fountain Token Distribution

Following the airdrop, we will launch a Public Fountain for token distribution, earmarking 70% of our total token allocation for this phase. This inclusive initiative is structured to ensure a broad and equitable distribution, incorporating a single wallet limitation to foster widespread participation. Additionally, as a gesture of our continued commitment to the Motoko Ghost community, holders will receive bonus tokens, further enhancing their engagement and investment in our ecosystem.
